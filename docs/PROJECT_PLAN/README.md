## Project Overview

**RehaAdvisor** is a comprehensive Tele-Rehabilitation Platform designed to revolutionize remote rehabilitation management through intelligent intervention delivery, real-time patient monitoring, and evidence-based therapy tracking.

### Core Purpose
To provide therapists, researchers, and healthcare administrators with a unified digital platform that:
- Delivers personalized rehabilitation interventions to patients remotely
- Tracks patient adherence and health outcomes in real-time
- Integrates with wearable devices (Fitbit) for continuous health monitoring
- Facilitates data collection for clinical research
- Enables secure communication between healthcare providers and patients

### Primary Use Cases
1. **Patient Rehabilitation Management** - Therapists assign and monitor personalized intervention plans
2. **Remote Therapy Delivery** - Patients receive, execute, and provide feedback on exercises
3. **Health Data Integration** - Automatic collection of vitals, activity, and sleep data
4. **Clinical Research** - Researchers analyze intervention effectiveness and patient outcomes
5. **Administrative Oversight** - Administrators manage users, content, and system operations

## Vision & Mission

### Vision
To empower patients, therapists, and researchers through intelligent, accessible, data-driven rehabilitation that breaks geographical barriers and improves health outcomes for gerontechnology populations.

### Mission
Provide an intuitive, secure, and scientifically-validated tele-rehabilitation platform that enables:
- **Patients**: Easy access to personalized therapy with progress tracking
- **Therapists**: Efficient management of multiple patients with data insights
- **Researchers**: Rich datasets for evidence-based therapy optimization
- **Administrators**: Complete system governance and user management

## Key Objectives

### 1. Patient-Centric Rehabilitation
- [ ] Enable flexible, accessible therapy delivery from any location
- [ ] Provide real-time feedback and progress visualization
- [ ] Integrate health data (activity, sleep, vitals) for holistic view
- [ ] Support multiple interaction modalities (video, audio, text)

### 2. Clinical Efficacy
- [ ] Enable therapist monitoring of patient adherence
- [ ] Collect comprehensive health metrics and therapy outcomes
- [ ] Support evidence-based intervention recommendations
- [ ] Facilitate clinical research through structured data capture

### 3. Operational Efficiency
- [ ] Streamline therapist workload through automation and templates
- [ ] Reduce administrative overhead through user self-service
- [ ] Ensure HIPAA compliance and data security
- [ ] Scale to support thousands of concurrent users

### 4. Data-Driven Insights
- [ ] Aggregate de-identified data for research
- [ ] Provide predictive analytics for intervention effectiveness
- [ ] Enable continuous quality improvement through metrics
- [ ] Support personalization through machine learning readiness
