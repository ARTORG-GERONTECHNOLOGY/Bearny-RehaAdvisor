Bearny

Be Ready After Rehabilitation — a digital telerehabilitation platform for patients and physiotherapists

Research platform

Bearny is a web-based telerehabilitation system developed at the ARTORG Center for Biomedical Engineering Research, University of Bern. It connects patients recovering from surgery or illness with their physiotherapists through personalised exercise plans, remote progress monitoring, and standardised clinical assessments.

What Bearny does

  • Delivers personalised exercise and education programmes to patients at home
  • Allows physiotherapists to monitor patient progress remotely via a clinical dashboard
  • Collects patient-reported outcomes and standardised assessments (e.g., PROMS)
  • Integrates with wearable devices (Fitbit / Google Health) to display activity, sleep, and heart rate data
  • Supports multi-language use across clinical research sites in Switzerland and beyond

Who uses it

Bearny is used in approved clinical research studies and telerehabilitation programmes at university hospitals and rehabilitation centres. Access is by invitation from a treating physiotherapist or research coordinator — the platform is not open for public self-registration.

Data & privacy

All data is stored on servers located in Switzerland and processed in compliance with Swiss and European data protection law (nDSG / GDPR). Wearable health data connected via Google Health is accessed in read-only mode and is never sold, shared, or used for advertising. See our full Privacy Policy for details.
